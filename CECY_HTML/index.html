Grupo Arhe
Grupo Arhe is a conglomerate of companies with participation in the Tourism, Services and Infrastructure sectors, whose intention is to generate value to its different audiences through unique experiences and integral solutions. We design a brand that projects its leadership in these industries and its responsibility to society and its environment.

CR

Cecilia Rodríguez is currently Design Director at MBLM MX. She has developed in the professional field since 2011 specializing in brand creation/brand development. She holds a bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design awarded by Universidad Anáhuac campus Tampico and a diploma in UX/UI design awarded by Ironhack.

From 2011 to 2014 she was part of the creative team of MBLM MX (Junior Designer & Designer) developing brand creation for clients such as Mabe, Grupo Modelo, Yucatán’s tourism brand, Cultiba (holding with majority stake in Grupo GEPP - exclusive bottler of PepsiCo), among others.

2011-2014

From 2014 to 2015 she was part of the creative team of Diecinueve36, venturing into the mexican film and television industry by creating brands with national exposure such as PM Canal 5 (Televisa) and Yoga con Luz (Unicable) and participating in films title sequence design such as Alicia en el país de María, A la mala, Visitantes, Amor de mis amores, among others.

2014-2015

From 2015 to 2017 she worked as Senior Designer at FutureBrand MX developing brand strategy & identity for clients such as Disensa (Lafarge Holcim), Trend Group, GEPP, Helados Pingüino (Unilever), Engenium Capital (former GE Capital), among others.

2015-2017

From 2017 until today she’s been leading strategic design projects for clients such as Universidad Anáhuac, CFE, Totalplay, Banco Santander, OneMetro and La Madrileña.
